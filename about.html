About Us

Our Mission

Millions of girls are being denied an education across the globe. According to UNESCO, 31 million girls of primary school age aren't in school in addition to the 34 million adolescent girls. This lack of education has lifelong impacts. Around the world, two thirds of the 774 illiterate people in the world are women.

By improving access to education, rates for early motherhood are lowered, reducing mother & infant mortality. Young women can find work for themselves and become more independent. As such, e're aiming to improve access to education for girls and women all around the world.

How We Started

We came up with this idea at Equithon 2018 hosted at the University of Waterloo. Access to education and women empowerment are both topics our team is extremely passionate about, and we hope we can use this opportunity to make a difference.

How Are We Planning On Doing This?

We realize this is no easy task. Not only do we have to compile the necessary educational resources and find mentors, we also have to get this information out. This website will be our base, but we also have plans to eventually utilize radio/cellphone networks to broadcast podcasts and info sessions. Ideally, we will be able to create community hubs where girls and women can come together, support each other, and work towards achieving their goals.
